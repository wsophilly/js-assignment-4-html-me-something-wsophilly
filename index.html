Jevon's 1st Haircut Experience 🦁

This is a story of how a boy becomes a man 😂 😂 😂

The Beginning

THIS IS THE BEGINNING OF JEVON’S 1ST HAIRCUT EXPERIENCE.

Jevon has realized that his Dad (whom he likes) has hired some clown to do something to him but he is not exactly sure what it is.
;

Jevon Begins Processing

JEVON IS NOT HAPPY AND HE IS ALSO AN EAGLES FAN.


It is not lost on Jevon that the Clown his Dad has hired is a Cowboys Fan (as noted by the stop sign decal on the wall).

Jevon is processing everything that is wrong with what is happening in his life right now - as the haircut begins.

(In hindsight if his Dad had realized said clown was a Cowboys Fan, he would not have hired him either.)

Jevon Decides to Stare At the Clown


Halfway during the haircut, Jevon decides to stare at the clown very intently. We can only assume he is trying to make sure he remembers exactly what he looks like.

NO HE DIDN'T 😤


Did the Clown attempt to use Alcohol on a Toddler? Jevon has never been more disgusted in his life.


THE LOOKAWAY

Jevon had decided to look away and plot his revenge.

He has decided that he will become a gangster. He's not sure what type of gangster, maybe a Blood or a Crip, or if he can somehow join the Mafia, but he knows he has options and his mind is made up.

He will be a gangster.

In His Feelings 😡

Jevon is clearly scarred (emotionally) and has decided to look back at the clown one last time.

IF I WAS THE CLOWN I WOULD PACK MY BAGS.

IT WAS AT THIS MOMENT

Jevon realizes that not only was gonna have to whack the Clown when he grows up,

but he will also have to murder his Dad as well.

While he is definitely angry enough to do it, it also made him sad (cuz he has a brother and 2 sisters).

LOOKING IN THE MIRROR

After some Coaxing and looking in the mirror at his fresh new cut, Jevon decides to smile.

He has been told that the Experience is over.

Minutes Later .... However

Jevon realizes that he is still a Made man and one day ....
HE’S GONNA HAVE TO DO WHAT HE’S GOTTA DO.

The Bribe and Selfie

Later that evening after the trauma has worn down, Jevons Dad offered him a very well timed Bribe of a vanilla Shake and McDonalds fries. The plan worked and Jevon poses and smiles for a selfie with his Dad.

All plans to one day murder his father were called off.

The Clown / barbers life is spared as well but conditional on never seeing him on the street again.

7 YEARS LATER

In order to protect innocent Barbers / Clowns from catching a stray, Jevon's Dad learned it would wiser to learn how to cut his hair himself.

He spend $400 on clippers during the pandemic and watched a ton of Youtube videos.

He now cuts his son's Hair every other week in their own home. He is not very good yet but he has a couple of years before his son really cares who cuts his hair. He even made a 1 min Tik Tok style video to see if it would be a viable business venture one day.

THE END
